# Lakeraven Partnership

**As of Feb 2026:** We are bringing Lakeraven into the homelessness projects (Santa Fe, Albuquerque, and related work).

---

## Who Lakeraven is

[Lakeraven](https://www.lakeraven.com/) is a **Native-owned, veteran-led** digital services consultancy. They focus on:

- Human-centered digital services for the public good
- State, local, and tribal government
- Strategy, delivery, and modernization (agile, user-centered design)
- Accessibility (Section 508, WCAG 2.2 AA)
- Custom application development
- Product and UX strategy
- Capacity building and enablement

Their team is largely **former federal technologists** (18F, TTS, Presidential Innovation Fellows) with experience modernizing mission-critical systems for federal agencies, states, and tribal governments.
